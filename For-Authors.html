PRACTICAL APPLICATION OF BLOCKCHAIN TECHNOLOGY FOR ADDRESSING ENVIRONMENTAL PROBLEMS IN THE WASTE RECYCLING CYCLE

Author's Instruction and Rules for Submissions

  1. All submissions must be original and should not have been previously published or currently under review by another conference or journal.
  2. Submissions must be in English and follow the formatting guidelines provided on the conference website.
  3. Papers must not exceed 10 pages, including all figures, tables, and references.
  4. Submissions should be in PDF format and should be submitted through the online submission system on the conference website.
  5. The submission should include a title page with the title of the paper, authors' names and affiliations, and contact information for the corresponding author.
  6. The paper should include an abstract of no more than 250 words and keywords.
  7. All references must be cited using the IEEE referencing style.
  8. Authors are responsible for obtaining permission to use copyrighted material in their papers.
  9. All submissions will undergo a double-blind peer-review process, and the authors will be notified of the outcome of the review.
  10. At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present the paper at the conference.

Important Dates

  • Paper submission deadline: 30.03.2023
  • Notification of acceptance: 31.03.2023
  • Camera-ready submission deadline: 01.04.2023
  • Conference dates: 03.04.2023

Note: Any papers that do not comply with the above instructions and rules may be rejected without review.
