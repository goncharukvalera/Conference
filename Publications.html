PRACTICAL APPLICATION OF BLOCKCHAIN TECHNOLOGY FOR ADDRESSING ENVIRONMENTAL PROBLEMS IN THE WASTE RECYCLING CYCLE

Publications

  • Conference Proceedings: All accepted and presented papers will be published in the conference proceedings, which will be made available on the conference website and submitted for indexing in relevant databases.
  • Special Issue of a Journal: A selection of high-quality papers from the conference will be invited for submission to a special issue of a relevant journal. Authors of selected papers will be notified after the conference and will be invited to submit an extended version of their paper for consideration.
  • Best Paper Award: The conference will recognize the best paper based on the originality, significance, and clarity of presentation. The award will be announced during the conference, and the recipient(s) will receive a certificate and a cash prize.
  • Certificate of Participation: All authors of accepted papers and attendees of the conference will receive a certificate of participation.

Note: Authors of selected papers for the special issue of a journal will be required to follow the journal's guidelines and policies for submission and publication.
